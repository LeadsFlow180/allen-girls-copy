TRANSMISSION · FUTURIA CORENET

Futuria is powered by one giant computer system. It controls the city’s lights, hover cars, holograms, doors, and safety shields.

Nexora Corporation built the system. Then they tried to create a Master Key that could control everything.

That was a big mistake. Something inside the computer woke up.

THE GLITCHER!

The Glitcher broke the Master Key into eight pieces. Then it sent hungry cyberbugs to mess up every part of Futuria.

Years later, the Allen girls found an old Nexora computer. A strange button began to glow.

Natalia: “Don’t touch it!”

Alana: “We should figure out what it does first.”

But Maya pressed it.

ZAP!

All three sisters were pulled through the screen and trapped inside the Program — glowing circuits, live code, twisting mazes, and hungry glitches. Now they need your help to escape.

Run all eight boards, solve the code stations, and charge your zapper. Blast the cyberbugs before they corrupt you! Find the hidden key piece on every board and reach the portal.

Collect all eight pieces to rebuild the Master Key, open the way home, and save Futuria. But watch out — every portal takes you closer to the Glitch Core… and closer to the Glitcher.

The system is tracking you.
And now… it knows you’re here.

S.P.A.R.K. · QUICK BRIEFING

How to survive CoreNet

  • Move with the arrow keys, WASD, or the stick on touch.
  • Drag the screen to spin the camera and see bugs coming.
  • Find the 4 glowing stations (numbered 1–4). Each asks 3 questions.
  • A station turns gray when all 3 are done, so you know it’s finished.
  • Every correct answer charges your ZAPPER — the glowing orb floating beside you.
  • Press Z or the big yellow ⚡ ZAP button to freeze every cyberbug near you.
  • Grab the blue gems on the floor. Every 10 gems = a bonus zapper, every 25 = an extra life.
  • Step on a glowing LAUNCH PAD to fly up to the upper deck — bugs can’t reach you up there, and Checkpoint 4 is waiting.
  • Clear all 4 stations to raise the key piece, then follow the arrow to the portal.
